What is it?

The Dabloon Bank Discord bot is a bot which has been designed to be a new crypto currency for Discord. Using the bot you can view your balance, make payments, and view all of your recent activity.

Why?

The Dabloon Bank was created to allow its users to quick and easily create payments to other Discord users without the need for any 3rd party application.



Frequently Asked Questions

Whats the point of it?

The point of the Dabloon Bank is to allow users to easily create payments and transactions with other Discord users.

Is it secure?

Yes, as long as your Discord account doesn't get hacked, no one other than you will be able to perform transactions.

Is there 2 Factor Authentication?

Currently no, but we wish to add two factor authentication in the future.
